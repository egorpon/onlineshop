FAQ's

To contact the management, write an appeal using the feedback form in your personal account, subject: Feedback to the management → Contact the management.

If a store is blocked for low success rates, it will be automatically unblocked at the end of the specified blocking period (only if the seller has provided information on how he will improve the store). If the seller is blocked due to an unresolved request from the buyer, it will be activated only after resolving the issue with the client.

The product must be sold at the price that was indicated at the time of placing the order. In the absence of such an opportunity, it is necessary to inform the buyer about this by telephone and cancel the order by selecting the status “Cancel. Incorrect price on website.

Payment of generated invoices is not mandatory. In case of an error, you can create a new invoice and pay for it.

After the termination of the agreement, the funds remaining on the balance will be automatically returned within 20 working days to the settlement account of the sole proprietor / legal entity, which is indicated in the details of the agreement. Refunds of suspended sole proprietorships (if your account is closed) are carried out only on personal cards in IBAN format and personal income tax 18% and BC 1.5% will be taken into account from the refund amount.
